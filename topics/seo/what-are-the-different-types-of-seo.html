What are the different types of Search Engine Optimization (SE0) ?

Search Engine Optimization

SEO is the acronym for Search Engine Optimization. The process of SEO is the very foundation of digital marketing and there are a myriad of options when it comes to optimizing your websiteâ€™s traffic.

The 3 main types of SEO you need to know to master the fundamentals of SEO are :

  • OnPage SEO
  • OffPage SEO
  • Technical SEO
On Page SEO

While SEO is for the whole website, On page SEO focuses on the macro part. On page SEO requires you to focus on the page itself. You need to optimize each page to contain relevant keywords, titles and headings.



Images always relate better than words to the readers. But it does not mean the images can be poorly optimized. Images should have the exact keywords, lower file size, high resolution which makes it easier for the search engine to understand and pull it up for viewing.

The content should not be too long or too short. And too much use of keywords may result in less traffic. When the content of a single paragraph is too long, Google usually cuts off midway and this will drastically affect your website when crawling.

Title and meta description are the two main words which govern On page SEO.

Title should be between 10 to 70 words and the meta description which appears just below the title must be between 150 to words. Title and meta description are the first thing which the viewer sees and reads on first sight. So they must contain the best and most important points without giving away the content. In short, they must contain a hook so the viewers click on the link.

Off Page SEO

While Search Engine Optimization is to improve on the content of the website, Off page SEO can be very useful to improve the overall reputation and recognition of your website.

The main word to understand about Off Page SEO is Link Building. Link Building allows the search engine to know that your website is quite popular as it has links to other websites and can be trusted.



This can be done using backlinks (A link on a website to another website). The backlinks can be used anywhere (including social media) and are a great way of getting free promotion. Google can recognize these algorithms and can pull up your website more often.

Backlinking can be done to various websites which can be related to your website. For example, if your website is about an online education service, the backlinking can be used to link in pdf readers, video players or other educational products.

Similar to backlinking, Internal linking can help build the page get more sorted out and arranged. In addition to being a great way of navigation, it can be used to browse more information on your website. It also denotes the most important pages in your website to the search engine. A great example of internal linking is Wikipedia, with numerous links to other articles within its website.

Technical SEO

As the name suggests, the Technical SEO is part of the SEO you need, to handle the technical aspects of a website.

The user interface is as important as the content you are presenting. Without a proper user interface or backend foundation, the search engine crawlers cannot crawl through your website affecting data traffic.

A good website should have the following optimized :

  • Robots.txt
  • URL SEO
  • Overall speed of the website
  • Easy Navigation
  • Mobile Site

With this, you have the foundation of SEO. Now to put all the things you have learned to use. When you combine and use all the types of SEOs together, that is when your website can reach maximum amount of traffic.
