What is Search Engine Optimization (SEO) ?

Search Engine Optimization

Search Engine Optimization is the process of gaining more traffic from different search engines through organic search results and quality content. SEO is more targeted for organic traffic.

Advantage of SEO
  • Increase Traffic
  • No Inverstment Needed
  • Make profit with Adsense
  • Results will be pure Organic
Disadvantage of SEO
  • Initial Results take time.
  • More Competitive
  • Guarentee of results are quite low
  • Uncertainty in SEO algorithms

How Search Engine Optimization (SEO) works ?

When an Internet user searches for a keyword, or a phrase or a search term in search engines like Google, Bing, and Yahoo and the results provided against the query are a collection of relevant websites. But, the relevancy is not just enough to place your website on the first page of Google, there are many factors and some important of them are

  • Domain Authority (DA)
  • Page Authority (PA)
  • Page Ranking (PR)
  • Trust Score (TS)

Every search engine has their own search algorithms and factors that should be met for our websites to better rank in SERPs. an average of 85% uses google as their search engine for searching their queries.

Google's each algorithm works under a criteria.
