How to write quality content for SEO ?

Quality content writing

The basics of content writing are very simple. All you have to do is follow a few steps and you will be fine. Before going into detail about quality here are a few fundamental tips to keep in mind while content writing

An eye-catching title

Almost 90% of readers read the title and judge the content. The title must not be too long or too short. It should give just the right amount of information and should also be a hook to read the rest of the content.


Top Volume Keywords

The keyword is the most important factor when content writing. It is the sole factor in making or breaking your website. The traffic you receive is directly tied to the keywords used in your content.

There are many keyword tools that can help you find keywords related to your product.

For example, when your keyword or the main word of your content is cake, the keyword tool will give you outputs of other keywords like banana cake, cream cake, butter, etc. Add these extra keywords to further optimize your content.

Quality Unique content

Similar to the title the content must not be too short. Each paragraph must be spaced properly and sensibly. Complicated words and big paragraphs further decrease the chance of an average user. Once you understand the basics, here are a few overlooked and underestimated tips for a quality content



Stick to the point

Each piece of content must be about one thing and one thing only. Do not talk about two or more subjects in the same content.

While reviewing or editing the content, make sure that the content is about a single subject, and how it addresses the content.

Do not go about rambling or off-topic. The content must be crisp and on point.

Reviewing the Content

Keep editing the content. Edit as many times as possible. Each time you edit or review your content, it gets better.

Do not worry about the order or arrangement of the topic. Just write your ideas roughly and keep editing like a sculptor working on a piece of stone to turn it into exquisite art.

Research on Content

The groundwork or foundation you lay on will have an impact on the type of content you want to write.

Spend more time researching a topic than writing the content.

Keep easy to access notes to write your best ideas or lines whenever it comes up. Because ideas do not always pop up when sitting down to write. It is easier to go through already existing ideas rather than thinking of an idea on the spot.

Tools for Optimizing

Make use of all the free tools to further optimize your content.

A good tool to start out with is the keyword research tool ( Keyword Explorer) which gives you more keywords when you input a keyword. The extra keywords can be used in your content to boost traffic.

The readability test tool is another good tool that evaluates your content and gives a score. It also suggests a few improvements and rates the complexity of the content. If the sentences are too complicated with a high level of English words, the average user might find it hard to read.

A blog topic tool (Blog Topic Generator) can give you topics to work on when you are feeling dried out or cannot think of anything to write on. You just have to input a few keywords based on the topic and the tool gives numerous topic ideas.

Length of the content

The length of a content must depend on the context. To explain in detail, here is an example.

For the topic of ‘cake recipe’, the content cannot be more than 300 words because there is nothing more to write about other than ingredients and methods of preparation. An article with 500-750 words on the cake recipe will get less traffic than the content with 300 words because the short content has exactly what the user wants in a concise and crisp form.

These are some of the few tips and tricks which can further boost your content writing quality more than the usual tips seen everywhere. Make use of these tips and start writing.
